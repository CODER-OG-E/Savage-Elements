# Savage-Elements
A story-driven Unity strategy game where players rebuild humanity by solving global challenges inspired by the UN Sustainable Development Goals.
About the Game

Savage Elements is a low-poly 3D strategy game currently being developed in Unity.

Players take on the role of the **Global Sustainability Architect**, travelling across different countries to solve interconnected global problems through resource management, city building, and sustainable decision-making.

Planned Countries
- 🇰🇪 Kenya — SDG 2 & 3
- 🇯🇵 Japan — SDG 4 & 9
- 🇳🇱 Netherlands — SDG 12 & 13
